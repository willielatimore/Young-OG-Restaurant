Our vegetarian-friendly Portobello Mushroom Burger is a hearty and delicious option, featuring a marinated and grilled Portobello mushroom cap topped with fresh arugula, ripe tomatoes, and a tangy balsamic glaze.

Seafood Linguine - $16.99
